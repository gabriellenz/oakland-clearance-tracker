What is included

  • Fatal police shootings are not included in the arrest-rate calculation.
  • Race and ethnicity are shown only when a credible source reports them directly.
  • Police data helps check case numbers and dates, but it is not treated as a complete victim list.
  • Cases with no clear public arrest report are not counted as cleared.
