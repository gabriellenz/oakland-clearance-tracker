Data rules

Designed to avoid inflating the public rate or duplicating incidents.

  • Officer-involved fatal shootings are excluded from the main tracker.
  • Race and ethnicity are coded only when a credible source explicitly reports them.
  • Open Data rows are used for case reconciliation, not as a clean victim list.
  • Unknown arrest status is not counted as cleared.
